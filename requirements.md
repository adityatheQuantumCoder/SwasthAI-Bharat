## Overview
SwasthAI Bharat is an AI-powered, voice-first healthcare assistant designed for rural India to improve early diagnosis, risk-based triage, and treatment adherence in low-resource settings.

## Functional Requirements
- Voice-based symptom input in local Indian languages
- AI-based health risk classification (Low / Medium / High)
- Automated follow-up reminders via voice calls and SMS
- Dashboard for ASHA workers to assist patients
- Doctor dashboard for reviewing patient risk summaries
- Integration readiness with Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM)

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Low-bandwidth and offline-tolerant operation
- High availability and scalability
- Secure handling of health data
- Explainable AI outputs for healthcare decisions

## Assumptions
- Users have access to basic mobile phones
- ASHA workers assist patients with limited digital literacy

## Constraints
- Limited internet connectivity in rural areas
- Resource-constrained healthcare facilities

## Success Metrics
- Increase in early diagnosis rate
- Improvement in treatment adherence
- Reduction in unnecessary hospital visits
